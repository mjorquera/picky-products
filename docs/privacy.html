Picky Products

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Picky Products is a personal content automation tool that creates and schedules Pinterest pins for an affiliate marketing account. It is not a public-facing product or consumer service, and it does not interact with or collect data from any end users.

Data we collect

This tool operates exclusively on behalf of the account owner. The following data is used during operation:

  • Pinterest account tokens — used to authenticate API requests to the account owner's own Pinterest Business account. Stored locally on the developer's machine and never shared with third parties.
  • Notion workspace data — product names, pin titles, descriptions, and affiliate links stored in the developer's own Notion workspace. Accessed via Notion's official API.
  • Amazon product information — publicly available product data (titles, images, pricing) from Amazon UK, used solely to generate pin content.

How data is used

All data is used exclusively to generate and publish affiliate content to the account owner's Pinterest account. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any party other than the integrated services listed below.

Third-party services

This application integrates with:

  • Pinterest API v5 — to create and schedule pins on the account owner's Pinterest Business account
  • Notion API — to read and update content in the developer's own Notion workspace
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate programme for product monetisation

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy and terms of use.

Data retention

No data is retained by this application beyond what is stored on the developer's local machine and their own Notion workspace. No external databases, servers, or cloud storage are used by this tool.
